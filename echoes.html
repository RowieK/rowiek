Semester Reflections

Semester 1 — Foundations

Focus: planning, research, prototypes.

Established goals, researched tech stack and built early prototypes to validate architecture and scope for Echoes from the Shadows.

Semester 2 — Development

Focus: core features, CI, iteration.

Focus: UX, accessibility, performance.

Refined UI, improved accessibility, and optimized load/performance; adjusted interactions based on usability testing.

Semester 4 — Reflection

Focus: documentation, retrospectives, next steps.

Compiled documentation, wrote technical retrospectives, and planned future enhancements and research directions.
